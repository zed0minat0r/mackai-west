How we work

Four stages. No surprises.

  1. 01

    Discovery

    We sit with the hiring leader — sometimes the CFO, sometimes the Tax Partner — and write the search the way a candidate would actually read it.

  2. 02

    Search

    Targeted outreach to senior talent we already know, paired with active sourcing in the right specialty pockets. No spam, no scattershot.

  3. 03

    Placement

    A short, vetted slate. Real interview prep on both sides. Honest negotiation. The fee covers the work — not the volume.

  4. 04

    Aftercare

    First 90 days matter. We stay in touch with the candidate and the hiring leader, and we replace the placement if it doesn’t take.
